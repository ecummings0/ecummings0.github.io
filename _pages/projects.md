<b>Rosette MR Fingerprinting for T1, T2, T2*, and Fat Fraction Mapping (2020-2025)</b>
======
* Developed a trajectory, sequence, and reconstruction pipeline to simultaneously measure T1, T2, T2*, and fat fraction maps from a single-breathhold sequence to improve non-invasive quantitative assessment of disease</br>
* Techniques reduced required breathhold duration, improved spatial resolution, and reduced quantitative errors compared to previous approaches.</br>
* Optimized acquisition and reconstruction techniques for cardiac, liver, and carotid applications across 0.55T, 1.5T, and 3T systems</br>
* Investigated iterative and deep learning-based techniques to improve reconstruction quality</br>
* Presented findings at ISMRM Annual Meeting (2021, 2022, 2024, 2025), Low-Field Workshop (2022), RSNA Annual Meeting (2023), and SCMR Annual Meeting (2025)</br>
* Manuscript published in Magnetic Resonance in Medicine (2026)</br>

<b>MR Fingerprinting Development Kit (MRFDK) (2023)</b>
======
* Worked with the Siemens development team onsite in Erlangen to implement and test 3D prostate and free-running cardiac MRF sequences in the MRFDK framework </br>
* Provided feedback to the Siemens development team for improvement of the MRFDK</br>
* Prepared educational guides and presentations for the MIITT group on the use and potential applications of the MRFDK</br>
* Made a recommendation to Seiberlich lab whether to integrate it into the lab’s development workflow</br>

<b>Multipoint Reconstruction for Radial GRAPPA (2017-2022)</b>
======
* Worked with the through-time radial GRAPPA reconstruction algorithm to improve the speed and memory requirements for reconstructing highly undersampled data, giving clinicians more freedom to switch slice orientations during a real-time scan or interventional procedures</br>
* Approach reduced calibration time from 30s to 5s with minimal impact on image quality</br>
* Findings presented at the Annual Meetings of BMES (2017) and ISMRM (2018), and published in Magnetic Resonance in Medicine (2022)</br>
